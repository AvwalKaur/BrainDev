A centralized AI engine that monitors and coordinates all operational systems, making the supply chain more cost-effective and operationally efficient

Key

Features

Benefits to Walmart

Real Time Agent Orchestration

Walmart can monitor, control, and reroute delivery, inventory, and supply agents instantly. This improves response time, minimizes downtime, and maximizes operational agility across stores.

Role based Secure Access

Only verified Walmart staff can access the system based on their roles. This ensures operational integrity while protecting critical logistics data from unauthorized changes.

Centralised AI decision hub

One platform for all agent data, metrics, and actions — reducing fragmentation. This boosts efficiency by unifying decisions under one intelligent, coordinated AI layer.

Crisis Aversion and Control

The system enables one-click crisis response to shut down or reroute agents in emergencies. This rapid containment protects Walmart’s supply chain from cascading failures during disruptions.
